Common Questions

FAQ

Do you come to my location?

Yes — that's one of our main selling points. We'll load up the truck and come to your home, farm, shop, or even trailhead. Just let us know where you are when you reach out.

Can you fix my ATV frame?

Absolutely. ATV and UTV frame repair is one of our specialties. Cracks, bent tubing, broken mounts — send us a photo by text and we'll tell you what we can do and give you a price.

Do you take small jobs?

Yes, and we actually prefer them. We started this business to help real people with real problems — not just big industrial contracts. No job is too small.

How do I get a quote?

Easiest way is to text us a photo of what needs to be done along with your location. We'll get back to you with a ballpark price fast — usually within a few hours during business hours.

What if I have my own metal/material?

No problem at all. If you've already got the stock, we can work with it. Just make sure to tell us the material type when you reach out so we know what we're working with.

How long does a typical job take?

Depends on the job. A simple weld repair can be done same-day. Custom fabrication takes longer. We'll always give you a realistic timeline before starting so you know what to expect.
