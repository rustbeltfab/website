Everything We Offer

No job too small. No drive too far. If it's metal, we can handle it.

01

Custom Welding

Got something that needs to be built, joined, or reinforced? We weld steel, stainless, and aluminum — custom to whatever you're working on. Bring us a sketch or just explain it.

02

ATV Frame Repair

Cracked frame, bent tubing, snapped mounts? We specialize in ATV, UTV, and off-road frame repair and reinforcement. We'll assess it, weld it solid, and get you back on the trail.

03

Mobile On-Site Service

Can't haul it in? No problem. We load up the truck and come to you — your driveway, shop, farm, or trail. We serve all of West Michigan. Call and we'll work out the details.

04

Repairs & Modifications

Broken brackets, worn-out hitches, cracked equipment frames — we fix it. Ag equipment, trailers, tooling, garden structures, and more. If it's broken, we can probably fix it.

05

Small Custom Fab

Need a one-off bracket, guard, rack, or mount? We love small jobs. Bring us a sketch or just describe what you need — we'll figure it out together and build it right.

06

Farm & Home Metalwork

Gates, fencing, livestock equipment, garden structures, stairs, and handrails. Practical fabrication for everyday life in West Michigan — functional and built to last.
